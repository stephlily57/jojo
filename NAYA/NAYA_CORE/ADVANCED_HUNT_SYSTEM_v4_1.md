### Les 5 Niveaux de Discrétion

```
EVIDENT (Score: 85-100 | Signals: 10+)
├─ Clair, facile à identifier
├─ Visibilité maximale
├─ Approche directe possible
└─ Exemple: Profitable SaaS, +25% growth

DISCRETION LEVEL 1 (Score: 70-84 | Signals: 8+)
├─ Discret, attention requise
├─ Visible au marché, pas toujours compris
├─ Nécessite recherche basique
└─ Exemple: Stable agency, good margins

DISCRETION LEVEL 2 (Score: 55-69 | Signals: 6+)
├─ Très discret, expertise requise
├─ Nécessite connaissance du marché
├─ Pas visibilité externe
└─ Exemple: E-commerce niche, patience needed

HIDDEN (Score: 40-54 | Signals: 4+)
├─ Caché, découverte exceptionnelle
├─ Connaissance approfondie requise
├─ Réseaux spécialisés
└─ Exemple: Consulting stable, low growth

SECRET (Score: < 40 OR Unique signals)
├─ Ultra-secret, réseau premium requis
├─ Opportunités exceptionnelles
├─ Signaux uniques (network effects, etc.)
└─ Exemple: Marketplace en growth exponentielle
```

## 📋 TIER 3: GÉNÉRATION D'OFFRES NATURELLES

### 6 Types d'Offres Générées

#### 1. CASH INJECTION (24-72h)
```
Capital: $5K-$100K
Revenue Share: 15%
Timeline: 1-3 days
Use Case: Working capital, payroll, supplier
```

#### 2. REVENUE ACCELERATION (6 months)
```
Capital: $20K-$250K
Revenue Share: 20% of NEW revenue
Timeline: 6 months
Use Case: Hiring, market expansion, product
```

#### 3. GROWTH INVESTMENT (2 years)
```
Capital: $50K-$500K
Equity: 10-15%
Timeline: 2 years
Use Case: Scaling, team building, market dominance
```

#### 4. ACQUISITION (6-24 months)
```
Multiple: 3-5x revenue (based on growth)
Payment: All cash or structured
Timeline: 6 months due diligence
Use Case: Founder exit, consolidation
```

#### 5. REVENUE SHARING (6-24 months)
```
Share: 20-30% of revenue growth
Capital: Often none (profit sharing)
Timeline: Ongoing relationship
Use Case: Aligned mutual growth
```

#### 6. ASSET PURCHASE (3-6 months)
```
Currency: Technology, customers, brand
Multiple: 1-3x specific asset value
Timeline: 3-6 months
Use Case: Selective acquisition
```
