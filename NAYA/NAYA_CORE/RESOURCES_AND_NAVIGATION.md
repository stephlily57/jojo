## 📊 CHEAT SHEETS

### 10 Solvability Markers (Quick Reference)

```
1. Cash Flow (25%)         Is it cash-positive?
2. Recurring Revenue (20%) Is it sustainable?
3. Asset Value (15%)       What's the foundation?
4. Customer Loyalty (10%)  Will they stay?
5. Market Position (10%)   What's the moat?
6. Growth (8%)            Is it accelerating?
7. Owner Commitment (7%)   Will they stick?
8. IP (3%)                Competitive edge?
9. Leverage (2%)          Scalable?
10. Network Effects (0%)  Exponential potential?
```

### 5 Discretion Levels (Quick Reference)

```
EVIDENT (85+)          Direct approach, fast engagement
DISCRETION_LEVEL_1 (70+)  Industry knowledge, selective
DISCRETION_LEVEL_2 (55+)  Expert research required
HIDDEN (40+)           Network activation needed
SECRET (<40)           Elite discovery only
```

### 6 Offer Templates (Quick Reference)

```
QUICK_CASH_INJECTION (24-72h)
├─ Best for: Positive-cash companies
├─ Capital: $5-100K
├─ Revenue share: 15%
└─ Return: 1.2x (3 months)

REVENUE_ACCELERATION (6m)
├─ Best for: Growing, capital-limited
├─ Capital: $20-250K
├─ Revenue share: 20% of NEW
└─ Return: 3.1x (12 months)

GROWTH_INVESTMENT (2y)
├─ Best for: Scaling high-growth
├─ Capital: $50-500K
├─ Equity: 10-15%
└─ Return: 12x (36 months)

STRATEGIC_ACQUISITION (6-24m)
├─ Best for: Founder exit
├─ Multiple: 3-5x revenue
├─ Payment: All cash
└─ Return: 8.5x (24 months)

REVENUE_SHARING (6-24m)
├─ Best for: Aligned partnerships
├─ Capital: Flexible
├─ Share: 20-30% of growth
└─ Return: 2.5x (24 months)

ASSET_PURCHASE (3-6m)
├─ Best for: Asset-heavy businesses
├─ Currency: Tech, customers
├─ Multiple: 1-3x asset value
└─ Return: 5x (12 months)
```

### 5 Cash Horizons (Quick Reference)

```
24h  Unpaid invoices, immediate needs
48h  Early payment incentives, quick deployment
72h  Monthly revenue, rapid allocation
6m   Recurring revenue growth multiplication
2y   Strategic value realization
```
